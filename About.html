About Our Company

Welcome to Nya-Estaco Stores, your premier destination for high-quality agricultural products. Established with a commitment to excellence, we specialize in providing top-grade cereals, flour, grains, animal feeds, and spices to both wholesale and retail customers across Kenya.

With years of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for reliability and quality. Our products are carefully sourced from trusted farmers and producers, ensuring you receive only the best for your home or business needs.

At Nya-Estaco, we understand the importance of quality nutrition for both families and livestock. That's why we maintain strict quality control measures at every stage, from sourcing to delivery.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality agricultural products at competitive prices while maintaining exceptional customer service and building lasting relationships with our clients. We strive to be the most trusted supplier in the region.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of quality cereals and animal feeds in East Africa, recognized for our reliability, quality products, and commitment to customer satisfaction. We aim to support food security and agricultural development in our communities.

Our Values

Quality: We never compromise on product standards
Integrity: Honest and transparent business practices
Reliability: Consistent service you can depend on
Community: Supporting local farmers and businesses
