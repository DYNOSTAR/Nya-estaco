About Our Company

Welcome to Nya-Estaco Stores, your premier destination for high-quality agricultural products. Established in 2010 with a commitment to excellence, we specialize in providing top-grade cereals, flour, grains, animal feeds, and spices to both wholesale and retail customers across Kenya.

What began as a small grain store in Nairobi has grown into a trusted supplier for homes, restaurants, and institutions throughout the region. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.

At Nya-Estaco, we understand the importance of quality nutrition for both families and livestock. That's why we maintain strict quality control measures at every stage, from sourcing to delivery. We work directly with trusted farmers to bring you the freshest products at competitive prices.
