Our Services

Muthokoi Processing

We specialize in processing muthokoi (hulled maize) to perfection. Our advanced machinery ensures the highest quality, making it ready for cooking or further processing.

Milling

Our milling services transform grains into fine flour for various uses. Whether it's maize, wheat, or sorghum, we ensure consistent quality and texture for all your milling needs.

Grains Cleaning

We provide professional cleaning services for grains, including sieving and destoning. Our process removes impurities, ensuring clean and safe grains for consumption or storage.

Storage

Our state-of-the-art storage facilities keep your grains and products safe and fresh. We offer both short-term and long-term storage solutions tailored to your needs.
