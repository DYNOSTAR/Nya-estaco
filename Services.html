Our Specialized Services

We offer comprehensive solutions to meet all your agricultural processing needs

Muthokoi Processing

We specialize in processing muthokoi (hulled maize) to perfection. Our advanced machinery ensures the highest quality, making it ready for cooking or further processing. We handle both small and bulk orders with strict quality control measures.

Learn More

Milling Services

Our modern milling equipment transforms grains into fine flour for various uses. We process maize, wheat, sorghum, and millet with consistent quality and texture. Custom grinding options available for specific flour textures.

Learn More

Grains Cleaning

Professional cleaning services including sieving, destoning, and sorting. Our multi-stage process removes dust, chaff, stones, and other impurities, ensuring clean and safe grains for consumption or storage. We handle all types of cereals and legumes.

Learn More

Storage Solutions

Climate-controlled storage facilities with pest control systems to keep your grains and products safe. We offer both short-term and long-term storage with flexible access options. Regular quality checks ensure your products remain in perfect condition.
